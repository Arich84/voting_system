Voting Rules and Guidelines

To ensure a fair and transparent election process, we have established the following rules that all participants must adhere to. These rules are in place to maintain the integrity of the vote and protect the rights of every voter and candidate.

1. Voter Eligibility

- Only registered users are eligible to vote. Please ensure you complete your registration before the deadline.
- Each voter is allowed to cast one vote per election cycle. Duplicate voting is prohibited and may result in disqualification.

2. Candidate Eligibility

- Candidates must submit their profiles and relevant information during the preparation period before the voting starts.
- Candidates must adhere to the guidelines set forth by the election organizers and maintain respectful conduct throughout the process.

3. Voting Process

- Voting will begin once the preparation period ends, and it will remain open for the specified duration.
- Votes are anonymous, and the system ensures that every vote is counted fairly.
- Once a vote is submitted, it cannot be changed or withdrawn.

4. Results

- Results will be displayed live on the platform once voting begins.
- At the conclusion of the election, the official results will be verified and announced.

5. Code of Conduct

- All participants, including voters and candidates, are expected to behave respectfully and adhere to the platform's terms of service.
- Any attempts to manipulate the voting system will result in immediate disqualification and further action if necessary.

Thank you for your participation and for upholding the integrity of the election process.
